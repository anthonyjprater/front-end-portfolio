About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am a passionate front-end developer who is dedicated to designing innovative and engaging user experiences. My expertise lies in creating clean, responsive, and intuitive interfaces that bring ideas to life. My goal is to build websites and applications that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing, and that engage users in meaningful ways.

Aside from my love for coding and designing, I also have a number of other interests that inform my work and bring balance to my life. I am an avid basketball player, weightlifter, sci-fi fan, and cook. I believe that my diverse range of interests and experiences enables me to approach problems and challenges from a variety of perspectives and come up with creative solutions.
